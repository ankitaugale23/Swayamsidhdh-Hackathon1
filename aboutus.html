Coordinator, Unnat Bharat Abhiyan

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to our Unnat Bharat Abhiyan website. The Unnat Bharat Abhiyan is a flagship programme of the Ministry of Human Resource development, government of India with intention to enrich rural India. The institute implement such great scheme with an adequate structural network and a proper mechanism to plan. The institute execute and monitor the UBA activities regularly to be able to create a tangible positive impact. The institute facilitate synergetic collaboration with all stakeholders. As per the directives of MHRD, government of India and IIT Delhi, the institute adopted the five villages in Pune districts and identify the problems and taken initiative to resolve the problems. Recently the soak pit construction project completed and solve the drainage problem and installation of RO water purifier of 1000 lit/hrs capacity in Malvandi village and gradually expand the out rich over the period of time. It also aims to create a vibrant relationship between the rural society and the institute. The students are also reoriented and connected to rural realities so that their learning and research work also become a more relevant to the society. The AISSMS COE is taking initiative and it is for the first time announced that the much awaited ATMANIRBHAR BHARAT: SWAYAMSIDDH VILLAGE HACKATHON 2020. Students from every corners of India will participate in a Hackathon at a large scale. Harnesses creativity & expertise of students, builds funnel for ‘SWAYAMSIDDH VILLAGE’ campaign, crowdsources solutions for improving governance and quality of life, and provides opportunity to students to provide innovative solutions to India’s daunting problems. The Management and principal approved a new program titled “Atmanirbhar Bharat: Swyamsidhha Village” Hackathon 2020 under UBA aimed at tapping the talent pool of engineering students, nationally and internationally to encourage their engagement with the our institutes and rural development in India so as to build ups the institute-society bonding, accelerate the pace of quality reform, and elevate India's technological capacity to rural excellence. During the HACKTHON 2020 teams of thousands of technology students will build innovative solutions for problems posed by Rural India. Winners stand to get cash prizes and a chance to be part of the incubation program. I, on behalf of AISSMSCOE family welcome all the stakeholders and wish you all the best for achieving greater success and scaling new heights in the coming “Atmanirbhar Bharat: Swayamsidhha Village” Hackathon 2020 under Unnat Bharat Abhiyan.
