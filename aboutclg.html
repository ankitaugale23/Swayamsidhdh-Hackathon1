All India Shri Shivaji Memorial Society

In the proposal, he declared his intention of creating a central hall with Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj’s life size statue and a hostel to accommodate a hundred Maratha students.  The institutes are committed towards creating a community which is vibrant and which provides a lifelong learning experience and professional development. Corporates, academicians, industrialists and the student community have lauded the efforts made by the All India Shri Shivaji Memorial Society to take the Society to unscaled heights. The Society is now poised to receive the President of India on December 28, 2013 to unveil the Statue of Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj, Rishi amongst the Raja’s, true Ryatecha Raja, Educationist, Social Reformer, Champion of the underprivileged, Architect of countless benevolent and innovative developmental Projects and acts, Great Visionary, Founder President of AISSMS, whose ethos, ideals and dreams All India Shri Shivaji Memorial Society is vehemently trying to realise to the fullest measure!

All India Shri Shivaji Memorial Society's College Of Engineering

The All India Shri Shivaji Memorial Society (AISSMS) was established in 1917 by Late H H Shrimant Shri Shahu Chhatrapati Maharaj of Kolhapur to promote the noble cause of education. The AISSMS COE, Pune is a co-education Institute established in 1992. The College of Engineering is affiliated to the University of Pune. It conducts AICTE approved courses leading to the degree of Bachelor of Engineering (BE) in eight engineering streams and Master of Engineering (ME) in Seven engineering streams.

The College has highly qualified and experienced faculty on its roll. The laboratory, computer and library facilities in all Departments are well developed. The College also has a Central Library and Central Computing Facility. The College gives substantial emphasis on the teaching learning process. The College also provides unlimited broadband internet facility to the students. Besides national journals, the international journals are made available to the students and the faculty. The teaching programme also gives emphasis on practical training and industry-institute interaction.

National Symposium: College organizes a National Symposium for professionals on Forefronts of Engineering. Symposia have been organized on the themes of such as Durability of Concrete, Advances in Bioprocess Engineering, Power for Sustainable Development and Advances in Automotive Engineering and many more.

AISSMS Engineering Today: College organizes National Level Students’ Symposium and Exposition every year, to provide opportunities for the development of the students in academic as well as co-curricular and extra-curricular activities.

Research & Development: Core and inter-disciplinary research is carried out in the identified fore-front areas. College has number of sponsored research projects undergoing.

National Service Scheme: The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a Central Sector Scheme of Government of India, Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports. It provides opportunity to the student youth of India to take part in various government led community service activities. The sole aim of the NSS is to provide hands on experience to young students in delivering community service. Since inception of the NSS in the year 2013, the almost all the students and faculties are volunteered to take part in various community service programmes. The motto of National Service Scheme is NOT ME BUT YOU. A NSS volunteer would have the exposure and experience to be an accomplished social leader, an efficient administrator and a person who understands human nature. The NSS team implemented various social projects in and outside of Pune city. The NSS volunteers received various awards form university and various organisation.

Unnat Bharat Abhiyan: Unnat Bharat Abhiyan’ is an initiative of the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD). Its 2.0 version was launched in the year 2018 and in the first cycle MHRD awarded the UBA to our Institute. The institute has adopted five villages in different parts of Pune districts. The institute developed good relationship with the adopted villages and providing the knowledge and technology support to improve livelihoods in rural areas. The institute identified the problem and implement the project to solve it. The RO plant is implemented for clean drinking water. The soak pit construction resolve the problem of drainage system and upgrade the capabilities present systems.

Sports and Cultural Gymkhana provides a platform to students to explore their potentials in extra and co-curricular activities in several cultural and sports areas. Our College students participates in various sports and cultural events organized by various colleges. The institute organizes an Annual Social Gathering and Excellence Award Ceremony “Shivanjali”, ‘Ashwamedh’: Inter – Collegiate Sports and Cultural Competition, “Shivaji Cricket Trophy”, “Shahu Football Trophy”, etc. Inter- Collegiate competition: The students are participated in various cultural events like Verve, Vedant, Purushottam Karandak , Firodiya Karandak , Crescendo, Milanze, Zest , Versatalia, MIT Summit, Sakal Karandak, Sarpotdar Karandak, Kaware Trophy etc.

Students’ Chapters: The institute having the various students chapter such as Institute of Engineers (India), IEI, Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers, IIChE, Computer Society of India, CSI, Indian Concrete Institute, ICI, Society of Automotive engineers, SAE, Institute of Mechanical Engineers, IMechE, Indian Society of Heating Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Engineers, ISHRAE, Theory of Inventive Problem Solving Techniques, TRIZ. Various technical activities are organized under the aegis of this chapter in above engineering programs.

Students’ Clubs: The institute having different clubs like Aero Design Club (Garudashwa), Robotics Club, Drone Club, Film Club organising multifunctional programme.

AISSM'S College Of Engineering

It is with great pleasure that AISSMS College of Engineering has completed Silver Jubilee of its establishment in 2017. The AISSMS, COE providing state of art facilities to students, faculty, and all stakeholders. It is a matter of pride that the college has been granted ‘A+’ grade in NACC accreditation in 2018. This could be possible with the hard work done by college staff and students under the guidance of visionary management. AISSMS COE as an outcome of academic excellence achieved in different branches of engineering. Faculty is actively involved in research and development. The teaching program not only focus on classroom but also gives emphasis on practical approach through industry-institute interaction and the field work. College organizes various national and international level conference, symposium, workshop, seminar, cultural, sports, artrival and socio-economic programs. Also students actively work for different Technical teams under various students chapter. The National service Scheme is the strongest unit in the college which strengthen the institute-society relationship. Since from its inception, the village development projects are implemented for enhancement of quality of life. The construction of water reservoirs, digital literacy, clean village drive, environment enrichment program, donation of computer to school, Nirmal Wari, Health check-up camp and blood donation camp, women empowerment, awareness program and national level street play competition etc. During the Covid-19 pandemic period the NSS and UBA volunteers worked on field as a COVID-19 YODHHA and help more than ten thousand needy people by distributing the Grocery Kits, Mask, hand sanitizer, food packets distribution, quarantine centre, UV thermometer gun, food to stray animal, police mitra, disaster management cell PCMC, Global Covid-19 awareness program, family adoption and started covid-19 helpline for needy people. The institute started the Unnat Bharat Abhiyan, which is a flagship program of MHRD, Government of India in the year 2017. The institute adopted the cluster of five villages in the different part of Pune districts under UBA. Institute involved all the faculty and students to carry out the studies of living conditions in the adopted villages, access the local problems and needs work out the possibilities of leveraging the technological intervention and implementation of government scheme and preparation of action plan for selected villages. The institute is closely coordinate with district administration, villagers and other stakeholders and will become a part of the process of development, planning and implementation. The college are implementing the projects to improve the basic amenities like education, health, women empowerment, clean drinking water and drainage system for villagers
